# Chicken Broccoli

tags:
- meal
- healthy

ingredients:
- 2_breasts chicken
- 1_piece broccolli
- 1_cup almonds
- 1_cup basmati rice
- some olive oil
- some soy sauce

steps:
- start preparing the rice [see](./rice.md)
- prepare the chicken and broccolli into small bits
- chicken into frying pan with olive oil and let it cook
- add the broccolli, the nuts and the soy sauce
- stir for a while
- serve

servings: 2
