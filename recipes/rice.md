# Rice

tags:
- side

ingrediends:
- 1_cup rice
- qb salt
- 1_clove garlic
- qb bay leaves
- olive oil

steps:
- olive oil, garlic and bay leaves into pan, full power
- stir, let it get hot
- add rice, stir a bit
- add water (double the rice amount), let it reach boiling point
- after, add salt, put in medium do not touch it for 15 min
- ready!

servings: 2
