Guacamole hotdogs

Ingredients

  • 2 unity avocatos
  • 1 unity lemon
  • 1 some garlic
  • 1 unity onion
  • 1 unity tomato
  • 1 some salt
  • 1 unity fries
  • 4 unity hotdogs
  • 4 unity bread

Instructions

  1. first, make guacamole
  2. take avocatos, smash them in bowl
  3. add lemon juice, garlic, onion, tomato juice
  4. smash
  5. grill hotdogs
  6. hotdog into bread, guacamole into bread, some fries on top
  7. serve
