Chicken Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 2 unity chicken breasts
  • 1 unity broccolli
  • 200 g almonds
  • 180 g basmati rice
  • 1 some olive oil
  • 1 some soy sauce

Instructions

  1. start preparing the rice
  2. prepare the chicken and broccolli into small bits
  3. chicken into frying pan with olive oil and let it cook
  4. add the broccolli, the nuts and the soy sauce
  5. stir for a while
  6. serve
